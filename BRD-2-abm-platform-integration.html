1️⃣ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ABM campaigns targeting HVAM accountant firms are exceeding CTR benchmarks — 41% of firms (1.4k) are engaging with ads, representing access to 80,000 decision-makers across 3,200 firms. However, engagement signals are siloed in the ABM platform: firms showing high intent are not contacted during the "golden window," contact extraction is fully manual, and ABM influence on pipeline cannot be measured. This BRD automates ABM-to-Marketo signal ingestion, contact extraction, scoring integration, and closed-loop attribution to unlock ABM as a scalable pipeline driver.

3️⃣ PROBLEM STATEMENTS

Problem 1 — Marketing:

I am a Marketing Campaign Manager · I am trying to measure ABM effectiveness and prove ROI · But engagement data is siloed with no connection to pipeline · Because ABM, Marketo, and SFDC are not integrated · Which makes me feel like I can't justify ABM spend or optimize targeting

Problem 2 — Sales:

I am a MM Sales Seller · I am trying to reach decision-makers at high-intent HVAM firms · But I don't receive ABM-engaged leads in a timely way · Because contact extraction is manual (CSV) with no automated routing · Which makes me feel like the 41% engagement rate is wasted — the golden window closes before I can act

Problem 3 — Marketing Ops:

I am a Marketing Ops Manager · I am trying to activate nurture for ABM-engaged contacts at scale · But I manually pull lists, extract contacts, and enroll in Marketo · Because ABM and Marketo aren't integrated · Which makes me feel like I'm the bottleneck in a process that should be automated

4️⃣ HYPOTHESIS

If we integrate ABM with Marketo/SFDC to automate signal ingestion, contact extraction, scoring, and attribution, then Marketing proves ROI, Sales receives ABM-engaged leads during the golden window, and nurture activates at scale, as measured by: (1) ABM-influenced new meetings, (2) ABM-influenced pipeline ($), (3) signal ingestion latency ≤30 min, (4) contact extraction automation rate ≥95%, (5) ABM-to-MQL conversion rate ≥10%

TPM Recommendations

  • Middleware layer over point-to-point: Centralized error handling, retry/DLQ management, and observability. Direct ABM → Marketo calls are brittle.
  • Version the signal schema: Handle unknown signal types gracefully (log and skip, don't fail).
  • Explicit decision-maker criteria: Define exact job titles/seniority. Set per-firm contact cap (max 50) to prevent data quality issues.
  • Keep the 40-pt ABM score cap: Without it, high-engagement firms with zero direct contact could flood Sales with unqualified MQLs.
  • Store raw attribution events in analytics warehouse (not just aggregates) to enable re-analysis under different models.
  • Build an integration health dashboard: Signals/day, extraction events/day, DLQ depth, p95 latency, Marketo API usage %. Define failure runbooks.
