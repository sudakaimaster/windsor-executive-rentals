## Background

The Money BU sales team needs a prospecting tool to identify net-new contacts at target accounts. Current options fall short: **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** is ineffective because the target segment (smaller businesses) has minimal LinkedIn presence, and **buyer intent data** is unreliable for these companies due to low digital footprints. The primary use case is **contact discovery**, not intent signals. ZoomInfo Copilot's contact database and AI-assisted prospecting directly address this gap. Given high license costs, this 60-day pilot will validate the tool against clear metrics before any broader investment.
