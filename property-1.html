About this place

Come and stay at this 5-star inspired home! Over 3,400 SQ FT of living space featuring high-end furniture, a King Bed with Beautyrest Harmony Lux Mattress, Hotel Collection towels, and high thread count sheets. Perfect for professionals, couples, and families.

The beautiful open concept space boasts 12-foot high ceilings throughout the entire unit with main level laundry. Enjoy 2 living rooms, 6 flatscreen Sony & Samsung TVs in every bedroom, a fully stocked kitchen, and a Keurig Coffee & Tea Bar with a large selection of pods and condiments.

Unwind in the private gym featuring a treadmill, free weights, bench, yoga mats, and a sauna. Stay entertained with board games, ping pong, and an arcade game room coming soon! Streaming includes Netflix, Disney Plus, Prime, and Cable TV. We provide premium toiletries including organic Crate 61 Soap and Chi Brand shampoo, plus refreshments to make your stay feel like home. We take pride in keeping the home spotless — everything is thoroughly cleaned & sanitized to perfection before your arrival. Guest has access to the entire property!
