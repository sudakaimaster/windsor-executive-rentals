About this place

Beautiful fully renovated two-bedroom home with high-end Gus Modern furniture, granite kitchen and laundry countertops, one-car garage, and high-end bedsheets with two queen-sized mattresses. Modernly designed and furnished — cleaned very thoroughly before every stay!

Conveniently located in downtown Windsor, just a few blocks from the Windsor-Detroit Tunnel. Walking distance to Caesars Windsor Casino, the scenic Detroit River, and Little Italy on Erie Street. Single-level home with no stairs — perfect for accessibility.

We pride ourselves on our cleanliness — we make sure to clean everything, even if it’s been put away or unused, before your stay. We treat your stay the way we would hope others would for us. The home features self check-in via smart lock, free parking on premises plus free street parking, a private backyard with outdoor furniture, in-unit washer & dryer, and a dedicated workspace. Family-friendly with crib, pack ’n play, high chair, baby bath, baby safety gates, and outlet covers available. Long-term stays welcome (28+ days).
